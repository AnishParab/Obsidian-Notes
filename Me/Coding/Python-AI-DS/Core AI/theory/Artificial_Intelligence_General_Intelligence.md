# General Intelligence

- Intelligence = ability of an agent to achieve goals across environments under uncertainty

**Core components**
- Perception — extract patterns from raw input
- Reasoning — draw inferences from available information
- Learning — update behavior from data/experience
- Planning — select actions toward a goal
- Generalization — perform on unseen cases

**Misconception**
- Intelligence ≠ pattern recognition — that's one component, not the whole

---
# Intelligence in AI

Current reality:
- AI ≈ statistical function approximation: f_θ(x) → y
- Optimizes a proxy objective over a fixed data distribution

What works:
- Pattern recognition
- Narrow task optimization

What fails:
- General intelligence (cross-domain goal pursuit)
- Causality (intervention reasoning)
- Long-term planning (beyond local optima)
- True creativity (output is recombination, not origination)
- Emotional intelligence (no internal states exist)
