# CIA Triad

- **Confidentiality** — only authorized parties can read data, e.g. account information
- **Integrity** — data cannot be altered without authorization, e.g. patient records
- **Availability** — data must be accessible when needed, e.g. authentication service

# Additional Elements

- **Authenticity** — verifying that entities are who they claim to be
- **Accountability** — actions must be traceable to the responsible entity
