# Cryptanalysis

- Science of breaking ciphers without the key

---
# Types (weakest → strongest)

- **Ciphertext only** — attacker has ciphertext only, uses frequency analysis and pattern matching
- **Known plaintext** — attacker has plaintext + ciphertext pairs, deduces the key
- **Chosen plaintext** — attacker chooses plaintext and obtains ciphertext, crafts inputs to expose key behavior
- **Chosen ciphertext** — attacker chooses ciphertext and obtains plaintext, works backwards to deduce key
- **Chosen text** — combination of chosen plaintext and chosen ciphertext attacks
