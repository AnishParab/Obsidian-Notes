# Spectrum Analyzer

- Converts audio frequencies into a real-time visual display
- X-axis = frequency (20 Hz – 20 kHz), Y-axis = amplitude (dB)
- Turns the auditory skill of hearing frequencies into a visual reference
- Helps identify problematic frequencies, resonances, and spectral balance at a glance

---
**Limitations**
- Analog EQs typically have no spectrum analyzer — what you see is just knobs and filters
- This is why ear training is non-negotiable — you won't always have a visual aid
- The analyzer assists the ear, it doesn't replace it
