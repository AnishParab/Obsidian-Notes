**Fun Fact**
- In the 40's and 50's Audio Engineers used to be called *balance engineers* because all they had was volume faders and no compression and reverb. 

---
# What to Balance in the mix ?

1. **Dynamics**
	- Compression

2. **Space**
	- Panning, reverb and delay

3. **Tone**
	- Frequency Spectrum
> Balancing the Tone is the only thing we will work in the *Equalizers* Section.
