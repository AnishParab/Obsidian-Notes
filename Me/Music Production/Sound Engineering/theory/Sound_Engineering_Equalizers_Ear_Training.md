## 3. The Vowel Technique: **To find the Problematic Area**

- When your voice produces vowel sounds, it changes the soni energy produced
- Note that these words should be said in a more white noisy way
- **Boot** - 250 Hz
- **Toe** - 500 Hz
- **Faaaaaa**  - 1 kHz
- **Bet** - 2 kHz
- **Beat** - 4 kHz
- **Kiss** - 6 kHz

> Your **tone** will match the frequency
