# Masking

- Masking = two instruments occupying the same frequency range, causing one to cover the other
- Result: loss of clarity, muddy mix — neither instrument sits well
- Most common in the low mids (200–600 Hz) — the most crowded region

---
**Masking EQ Fix**

- Identify which instrument owns that frequency range — give it priority
- Cut the same frequency on the other instrument to create space
- Technique: if kick owns 80 Hz, cut 80 Hz on the bass guitar (and vice versa)
- High-pass filter non-bass instruments — most don't need anything below 100–200 Hz
- Low-pass filter instruments that don't need air — clears the highs for what does
- Complementary EQ: what you boost on one, cut on the other — they fit like puzzle pieces
