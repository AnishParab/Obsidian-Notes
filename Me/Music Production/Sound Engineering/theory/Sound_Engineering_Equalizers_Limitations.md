# EQ Limitations

- **Bad recording**
If the source sounds broken (wrong mic, bad placement, clipping), EQ only makes the problem more audible

- **Bad arrangement**
If two instruments are fighting for the same sonic space by design, EQ is a bandage not a solution

- **Bad performance**
Timing, pitch, and feel issues are performance problems; EQ has no tempo or tuning correction

- **Bad volume balance**
If a track is too loud or too quiet relative to others, that's a fader problem not a frequency problem

- **Bad listening environment**
If your room lies to you, every EQ decision you make is based on false information
