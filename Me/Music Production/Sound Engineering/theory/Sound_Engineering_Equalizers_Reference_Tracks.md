## Step 1

- Check the **loudness** of all reference songs and your track.
- Set it to almost the same level.
- Note that: Only check your VU meter and not the DAW volume meters.

**Pugins**
- **Built-in for Bitwig**
	- **Loudness Meter** device — Bitwig's native LUFS meter, put it on the master track. Does integrated, short-term, and momentary LUFS.
- **Free plugins (LV2/VST3 on Linux)**
	- **Youlean Loudness Meter** — the go-to free option, very detailed, shows integrated LUFS, LRA, true peak. Has a Linux VST3 build.
	- **MvMeter2** — free, solid metering, works on Linux.
	- **LUFS Meter by Klangfreund** — clean UI, free version covers the basics.
- **Paid**
	- **iZotope Insight 2** — overkill for most, but best-in-class if you go deep into mastering.

> Note that the dynamic range for track might be more than the reference track as your track is not mixed properly.
