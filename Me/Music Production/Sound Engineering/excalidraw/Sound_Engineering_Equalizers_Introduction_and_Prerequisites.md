# EQ Basic Definition

> Volume control for frequencies — boost what serves the sound, cut what doesn't

- Messing around with the **frequency spectrum** of the mix.

---
**Tone**
- Tone = a blend of multiple frequencies happening simultaneously
- This blend is what makes a guitar sound like a guitar and not a violin

---
**Hertz Definition**
- Hz = speed of a sound wave
- Faster wave = higher pitch

---
**dB Definition**
- Decibels (dB) = unit of measurement for volume/loudness
- In EQ context: how much you're boosting or cutting a frequency

---
**Q Definition**
- Q = width of the EQ filter
- High Q = narrow band (affects few frequencies)
- Low Q = wide band (affects a broad range of frequencies)
