# Fast Fourier Transform

- Additive synthesizers require many individual sine wave oscillators to recreate complex sounds by applying the logic of the Fast Fourier transform

> The **Fourier transform** is a mathematical transform that can decompose the function of a complex wave into its component frequencies.

> The **Fast Fourier Transform** is an algorithm based on the original Fourier transform and more closely related to the discrete Fourier transform.
